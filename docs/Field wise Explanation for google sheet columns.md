**GARUDA** _(Web application)_ 

# **FIELD WISE EXPLANATION FOR GOOGLE SHEET COLUMNS** 

By, Tirupati District Police 

## **I. BASIC IDENTIFICATION MODULE** 

## **A. Sl. No. (Serial Number)** 

## **1.Importance** 

- Unique internal reference number for each suspect/consumer. 

- Prevents duplication of records. 

- Enables linking of records across PSs and cases. 

## **2.Expected Analytics** 

- Total suspects registered. 

- Year-wise / PS-wise growth trends. 

- Repeat offender identification. 

- Record duplication detection. 

## **3.Intelligence Use** 

- Creation of centralized district-level NDPS registry. 

- Quick retrieval during investigations. 

## **B. PS (Police Station)** 

## **1.Importance** 

- Identifies jurisdiction of the individual. 

- Enables territorial crime mapping. 

## **2.Expected Analytics** 

- PS-wise NDPS hotspots. 

- PS-wise arrested accused statistics. 

- High-risk police station identification. 

- Emerging drug-prone police station analysis. 

- One accused resident of one place / jurisdiction committing of crime in another jurisdiction. 

## **3.Preventive Intelligence** 

- Resource allocation based on drug activity. 

- Targeted surveillance in high-incidence PS limits. 

- Deployment of special teams. 

## **4.Predictive Intelligence** 

- Forecasting future NDPS activity areas. 

- Identifying migration of peddlers between PS limits. 

## **C. Name of Consumer / Suspect** 

## **1.Importance** 

- Core identity field. 

- Enables linking with FIRs, CDRs, bank accounts, social media, and intelligence databases. 

- Allocation of Unique ID for each accused. 

## **2.Expected Analytics** 

- Repeat involvement patterns. 

- Alias-based duplicate detection. 

- Network linkage between accused. 

- One accused resident of one place / jurisdiction committing of crime in another jurisdiction. 

## **3.Intelligence Use** 

- Association mapping. 

- Criminal network visualization. 

## **4.Predictive Use** 

- Identification of recurring offenders likely to re-engage in NDPS activity. 

## **D. Test Results (Positive / Negative)** 

## **1.Importance** 

- Differentiates actual consumers from suspects. 

- Scientifically validates consumption. 

## **2.Expected Analytics** 

- Percentage of confirmed consumers. 

- Area-wise drug consumption trends. 

- Drug prevalence ratio. 

## **3.Preventive Intelligence** 

- Identification of emerging consumption zones. 

- Monitoring of schools/hostels/colleges if clusters emerge. 

- For proceeding further to extract information about hierarchy (local seller) of drug offenders in the area. 

## **4.Predictive Intelligence** 

- Forecasting increase in drug abuse in specific localities. 

## **E. Category / Hierarchy of Suspect 1.Categories** 

- Consumer 

- Local Peddler 

- Supplier 

- King Pin 

- Transporter 

- Interstate King Pin 

## **2.Importance** 

This is one of the most important intelligence fields. 

## **3.Expected Analytics** 

- Drug supply chain hierarchy mapping. 

- From consumer proceeding further to extract information about hierarchy (local seller) of drug offenders in the area. 

- Identification of distribution networks. 

- Detection of operational structures. 

- Mapping of cell phone connections, conversations and connections. 

- Mapping of cell phone IMEI device connections, conversations and connections. 

- Mapping of bank accounts / transactions connections. 

- Mapping of UPI accounts / transactions connections 

- Mapping of Jio locations 

- Mapping of vehicles used 

## **4.Preventive Intelligence** 

- Focused action against all ranks of hierarchy like suppliers etc. rather than only consumers. 

- Identification of source-to-street distribution channels. 

- Detection of interstate trafficking routes. 

## **5.Predictive Intelligence** 

- Prediction of: 

   - Upcoming supply movements 

   - Expansion of peddler networks 

   - New distribution zones 

## **6.Advanced Intelligence Possibilities** 

- Network graph analytics. 

- Kingpin influence mapping. 

- Automated hierarchy detection. 

## **F. Father / Husband Name** 

## **1.Importance** 

- Assists in identity verification. 

- Useful in tracing family-linked offenders. 

- For establishing connections through cell phones 

- For establishing connections through IMEI 

- For establishing connections through bank account and transactions. 

- For establishing connections through UPI accounts and transactions 

- For establishing connections through vehicles involved /seized 

## **2.Expected Analytics** 

- Family-based criminal involvement patterns. 

- Repeat involvement within households. 

## **3.Preventive Intelligence** 

- Detection of family-supported trafficking operations. 

## **G. Age** 

## **1.Importance** 

- Identifies vulnerable age groups. 

## **2.Expected Analytics** 

- Age-wise consumption trends. 

- Youth involvement analysis. 

- Student-age drug abuse mapping. 

## **3.Preventive Intelligence** 

- School/college awareness targeting. 

- Youth rehabilitation planning. 

## **4.Predictive Intelligence** 

- Forecasting drug abuse spread among specific age categories. 

## **H. Gender** 

## **1.Importance** 

- Helps understand demographic spread. 

## **2.Expected Analytics** 

- Male/Female/Others involvement ratio. 

- Gender-specific trends. 

## **3.Preventive Intelligence** 

- Women-focused counselling/intervention programs. 

- Identification of women courier networks.

## **III. HIGH-END ANALYTICS POSSIBLE THROUGH THE WEB APPLICATION** 

## **A. Crime Network Analytics** 

The system can automatically identify: 

- Suppliers linked to multiple consumers. 

- Common transporters. 

- Shared mobile numbers. 

- Shared addresses. 

- Financial transaction links. 

## **Output** 

- Criminal network diagrams. 

- Syndicate structures. 

- Hidden associations. 

## **B. Geographic Intelligence (GIS Analytics) Possible Outputs**

- Route mapping. 

- Hotspot analysis. 

- Colony-wise prevalence. 

## **Preventive Use** 

- Dynamic patrolling plans. 

- Check-post deployment. 

## **C. Predictive Policing** 

Using historical data, the system may predict: 

- Future hotspot areas. 

- Repeat offenders. 

- Likely trafficking routes. 

- Emerging drug markets. 

## **Alerts** 

- New accused entering hotspot areas. 

- Multiple consumers linked to one address. 

- Frequent interstate movement.

By integrating:

- Geo location 

- CDR analysis

## **E. Financial Intelligence**
