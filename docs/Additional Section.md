## **AI-BASED ANALYTICS MODULE** 

## **1. Introduction:** 

To strengthen intelligence-led anti-narcotics enforcement, the proposed web application shall incorporate Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), and Advanced Data Analytics for predictive policing, offender behavior analysis, network detection, hotspot identification, and automated intelligence generation. 

The AI module shall assist investigating officers, intelligence units, narcotics enforcement teams, and district police in identifying hidden criminal patterns, repeat offender trends, interstate drug trafficking networks, and suspicious operational activities. 

## **2. Objectives of AI-Based Analytics** 

The AI analytics engine aims to: 

   - Predict emerging narcotics hotspots. 

   - Identify repeat offender patterns. 

   - Detect organized drug trafficking networks. 

   - Analyze movement and communication patterns. 

   - Identify hidden associations between accused persons. 

   - Generate automated intelligence alerts. 

   - Improve preventive policing and surveillance. 

   - Support strategic anti-drug enforcement planning.
